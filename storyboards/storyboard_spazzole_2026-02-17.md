# Storyboard - Spazzole parabrezza (6s)

╭───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╮
SCENE 1/6 | 6s | 16 words
╰───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╯

VOICEOVER:
"Senti questo rumore? O magari il parabrezza rimane tutto rigato quando piove? È chiaro: le tue"

ACTION:
Driver-side wiper blade scrapes across wet windshield leaving streaky lines, brief close look at scratched/striped glass surface as water beads distort visibility
[EMPHASIS: The streaks and scraping contact point between rubber blade and glass—make the problem instantly obvious]

╭───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╮
SCENE 2/6 | 6s | 16 words
╰───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╯

VOICEOVER:
"spazzole sono andate. Ma non preoccuparti, cambiarle è un gioco da ragazzi e oggi ti faccio"

ACTION:
Person holds an old worn wiper blade, flexes the cracked rubber edge to show damage, then gives a reassuring “no big deal” gesture while reaching for a new blade
[EMPHASIS: The worn rubber edge vs the new blade—clear before/after contrast to sell simplicity]

╭───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╮
SCENE 3/6 | 6s | 16 words
╰───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╯

VOICEOVER:
"vedere come si fa in meno di un minuto. Per prima cosa, solleva il braccio metallico"

ACTION:
Hands lift the wiper arm away from the windshield until it stands upright, showing the hinge and spring tension clearly as it locks in vertical position
[EMPHASIS: The wiper arm moving from flat to vertical—this is the first key step]

╭───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╮
SCENE 4/6 | 6s | 16 words
╰───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╯

VOICEOVER:
"in posizione verticale. Un piccolo consiglio da professionista: metti sempre un panno o uno straccio sul"

ACTION:
Hand places a folded cloth/towel on the windshield exactly where the bare wiper arm would hit, smoothing it flat to cover the contact area
[EMPHASIS: The cloth barrier on the glass—make the protective layer the visual star]

╭───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╮
SCENE 5/6 | 6s | 16 words
╰───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╯

VOICEOVER:
"vetro. Se il braccio dovesse scattare per sbaglio senza la spazzola montata, rischi di spaccare il"

ACTION:
Wiper arm is shown hovering above the cloth; hand briefly demonstrates the spring tension by letting it move down under control, then stops before impact to imply the snap risk
[EMPHASIS: The tension/snap hazard—arm + glass danger moment, with the cloth as the safety solution]

╭───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╮
SCENE 6/6 | 6s | 7 words
╰───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────╯

VOICEOVER:
"parabrezza, e noi vogliamo evitare danni inutili."

ACTION:
Hand presses the cloth firmly in place, then gently lowers the wiper arm onto the protected area, finishing with a clear “safe” thumbs-up style gesture
[EMPHASIS: The gentle controlled lowering onto the cloth—communicate prevention and care]
